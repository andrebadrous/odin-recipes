Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka is a middle eastern dish that is made by cooking a tomato based sauce with your choice of vegetables and seasoning, then cooking eggs on top of the sauce.

This dish is usually eaten straight out of the pan it was cooked in, with a side of pita bread as your utensil to eat the food.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • pita bread
  • onions
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat with a generous amount of olive oil
  2. Cut tomatoes into small pieces and place them into the pan stirring often
  3. Heat up pita bread
  4. Once it becomes sauce, crack eggs over the sauce and cover until eggs are fully cooked
  5. Enjoy
