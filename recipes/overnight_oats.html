Overnight Oats

Description

Rolled oats left overnight in the fridge with milk, chia seeds, and fruit to make a tasty and healthy breakfast

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Fruit
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Place oats in a jar
  2. Pour mild over oats
  3. Place chia seeds, fruits, and honey over oats
  4. Ensure all Ingredients are covered by milk and place the jar in the fridge
  5. Enjoy in the morning
